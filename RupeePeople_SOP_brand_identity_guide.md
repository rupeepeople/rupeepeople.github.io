🏛️ 1. Brand Essence
Brand Purpose
To help Indian families discover and recover hidden financial assets — safely, privately, and quickly.

Brand Promise
Clarity, recovery, and peace of mind during life’s most difficult moments.

Brand Values
Empathy — We support families with care and sensitivity.

Privacy — Every detail is confidential and protected.

Transparency — No hidden fees, no jargon, no confusion.

Accuracy — Evidence-based discovery across all financial systems.

Speed — Clear timelines and fast execution.

Brand Personality
Warm • Trustworthy • Professional • Calm • Intelligent • India‑first

🗣️ 4. Brand Tone & Voice
Tone Pillars
Empathetic
We speak gently, especially in post‑death and medical cases.

Clear
No jargon. No complexity. No confusion.

Confident
We know the process. We guide families with certainty.

Private
We reassure clients about confidentiality at every step.

Action‑Oriented
Every message leads to a next step.

Voice Examples
Post‑Death Case
“I’m very sorry for your loss. I’ll guide you step-by-step and make this process simple and stress-free.”

Medical Emergency
“I’m here to help you quickly. Let’s find every possible source of financial support.”

Job Loss
“We’ll help you recover PF, gratuity, ESOP, and pending dues — fast.”

Divorce/Legal
“Your information is confidential. We’ll help you identify hidden or undisclosed assets.”

🧱 5. Logo Usage Guidelines
Primary Logo
Deep green “R”

Black “RupeePeople” wordmark

Light beige background

Clear Space
Minimum padding: 1× logo height on all sides.

Incorrect Usage
Do not stretch

Do not rotate

Do not change colors

Do not place on busy backgrounds

Do not add shadows or outlines

“Your information stays private.”

“Handled only by authorized case managers.”

🧭 9. Brand Positioning Statement
RupeePeople is India’s trusted asset discovery service — helping families find hidden financial assets during life’s most difficult moments, with empathy, privacy, and accuracy.

📣 10. Brand Do’s & Don’ts
Do
Use green + black + beige consistently

Speak with empathy

Keep text short

Use checklists

Use WhatsApp-first CTAs

Highlight privacy

Don’t

Overpromise timelines

Use complicated financial jargon

Use aggressive sales tone
